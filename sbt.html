Healthy Weight Loss Guide

Simple, sustainable tips for your wellness journey

Set Realistic Goals

Aim for a healthy weight loss of 1-2 pounds per week. Rapid weight loss is often unsustainable and can be unhealthy.

Track Your Calories

Keep a food diary or use a calorie tracking app to understand your daily intake. Create a moderate calorie deficit of 500-750 calories per day.

Stay Active

Combine both cardio and strength training exercises. Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate activity or 75 minutes of vigorous activity per week.

Be Patient

Sustainable weight loss takes time. Focus on building healthy habits rather than seeking quick fixes. Celebrate small victories along the way!

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water throughout the day. Sometimes thirst can be mistaken for hunger. Aim for 8 glasses (64 oz) of water daily.
